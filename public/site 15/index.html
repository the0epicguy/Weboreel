Our Philosophy

Luxury is a
State of Mind

LUXE was founded in 2012 with a singular vision: to curate the world's finest luxury goods for those who value quality above all else. We don't chase trends — we recognize timelessness.

Every product in our edit is personally reviewed by our curation team against a 40-point quality standard. Material provenance, artisan credentials, ethical sourcing, and construction integrity are evaluated before a single item earns its place in our collection.

"We don't sell objects. We curate experiences that last a lifetime and stories worth passing down."— Isabelle Delacroix, Creative Director

Client Questions

Frequently Asked

How do you verify product authenticity? +
Every product undergoes a rigorous authentication process by our in-house specialists and independent third-party experts for high-value items. Each piece ships with a LUXE Provenance Certificate and unique QR code linking to its verification record.
What is your returns and exchange policy? +
We offer a 30-day hassle-free returns policy for all items in original condition with tags attached. Maison and Atelier members receive complimentary courier collection. All other returns include pre-paid return labels.
Do you offer international delivery? +
We deliver to 148 countries with full insurance coverage and real-time tracking. All customs duties and taxes are calculated transparently at checkout. White-glove in-room delivery is available in select cities.
Can I purchase as a gift? +
Absolutely. Select "Gift Order" at checkout to include our handwritten gift message service, choose from three premium packaging options, and opt for direct-to-recipient delivery with price concealment.
