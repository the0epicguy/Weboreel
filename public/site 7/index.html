The Fractured Age Begins

In the year 3000 AV, the seven Void Gates were unsealed by an unknown force. Dimensions collapsed. Reality bent. Ancient creatures awakened from their thousand-year sleep, hungry for the souls of the living.

You are a Riftwalker — one of the few beings capable of traversing the dimensional fractures. Your mission: restore the Eternal Seals before the Void consumes everything. But in the darkness, allegiances blur and corruption spreads even through those who seek to save the world.

// QUESTIONS

Need Answers?

Is VoidRealm free to play?+

VoidRealm offers a free base tier with access to 3 realms and limited character roster. Full content requires a one-time purchase. We have no pay-to-win mechanics — all competitive advantages come from skill only.

What platforms is it available on?+

PC (Steam/Epic), PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and Nintendo Switch (cloud version). Cross-platform play is supported across all platforms.

How large is the download?+

Base game is 87GB on PC, 74GB on consoles. We support streaming installation so you can start playing after 15GB is downloaded while the rest installs in background.

Is there a roadmap for future content?+

Yes! Year 1 Season Pass includes 3 major expansions and 6 free content updates. The Abyss never sleeps — we ship content every 6 weeks.
