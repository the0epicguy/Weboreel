FAQ

Common Questions

What makes NexaCore different from AWS SageMaker or Google Vertex? +
NexaCore is purpose-built for AI-native applications, not retrofitted cloud compute. Our unified API surface, built-in vector database, and multi-provider model routing eliminate the need to stitch together 8+ separate services. We're also 40–60% cheaper for equivalent AI workloads.
Can I use NexaCore with my existing infrastructure? +
Absolutely. NexaCore is designed to be composable. We offer Kafka-compatible streaming, S3-compatible storage, and OpenAI-compatible model APIs. Most teams are integrated within hours, not weeks, with no rearchitecting required.
How does data residency and compliance work? +
NexaCore offers data residency controls at the region, zone, and project level. For regulated industries, we provide HIPAA BAAs, GDPR DPAs, and can deploy into your VPC or on-premises environment for complete data sovereignty.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits? +
We never hard-limit or throttle production traffic without warning. Overages are charged at transparent rates published in your billing dashboard. We proactively alert you at 70%, 90%, and 100% of limits and can auto-upgrade your plan with your approval.
Do you offer a free tier or trial? +
Yes — our free tier includes 100K API requests, 500MB vector storage, and 1 model deployment forever. Pro and Enterprise come with a 30-day free trial, no credit card required.
