◈ THE LORE

Before Time
Had a Name

The Void existed before the universe. A formless expanse of raw dimensional energy, it consumed entire realities like a black hole consumes light — completely, without remorse, without end.

Then came the Fracture. A single crack in the dimensional membrane, and from it poured the first Void-touched warriors — beings neither fully material nor entirely lost to the darkness.

You are one of them. Born from the collision of existence and oblivion. Your only purpose: fight, evolve, dominate — or be consumed by the Void you claim to command.

◈ SUPPORT

Player FAQ

Is VoidRealm free to play? +
Yes, VoidRealm is completely free to download and play. All champions are unlockable through gameplay. Purchases are cosmetic only and have zero impact on competitive balance.
What platforms is VoidRealm available on? +
VoidRealm is available on PC (Steam and direct), PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and Nintendo Switch. Full cross-platform play and cross-progression are supported across all platforms.
Are there anti-cheat systems in place? +
VoidRealm uses a multi-layered anti-cheat system including kernel-level detection, machine learning behavioral analysis, and a dedicated Trust & Safety team. We have a zero-tolerance policy — bans are permanent.
How often is new content added? +
New seasons launch every 8 weeks, each introducing new champions, maps, and ranked mechanics. Hotfixes and balance patches are deployed weekly. Major expansions that add new game modes launch twice yearly.
