Our Philosophy

Material Honesty +

We do not hide structural concrete. We do not paint over raw steel. A building should express how it was built.

Light as Structure +

Natural light is our primary building material. We sculpt spaces to catch the sun's trajectory throughout the day.

Contextual Harmony +

A structure must not yell at its surroundings; it must converse with them.
